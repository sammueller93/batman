Story

After his parents were viciously murdered before his eyes, young Bruce Wayne swore to rid Gotham of its evil. He trained in the arts of combat and investigation and used his inherited billions to become a hardened vigilante. By day he is Bruce Wayne, rich playbody; but by night he is Batman.

Tools of the Trade

Batman has many tools to complement his own skillset. His famous utility belt holds batarangs, a grappling hook, and many other gadgets. He mostly gets around Gotham using his batmobile, but he also has motorcycles, a helicoptor, and a submarine.

The Look

Over the years, the Dark Knight's look has changed a lot. The original costume was moslty grey with yellow, black, and blue accents. As the content of the stories grew darker, so did Batman's costume. He has also gained more armor and function with the changes.
