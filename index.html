Story

After his parents were viciously murdered before his eyes, young Bruce Wayne swore to rid Gotham of its evil. He trained in the arts of combat and investigation and used his inherited billions to become a hardened vigilante. By day he is Bruce Wayne, rich playbody; but by night he is Batman.

Tools of the Trade

Batman has many tools to complement his own skillset. His famous utility belt holds batarangs, a grappling hook, and many other gadgets. He mostly gets around Gotham using his batmobile, but he also has motorcycles, a helicoptor, and a submarine.

The Look

Over the years, the Dark Knight's look has changed a lot. The original costume was moslty grey with yellow, black, and blue accents. As the content of the stories grew darker, so did Batman's costume. He has also gained more armor and functionality with the changes.

Allies

As powerful as he is, Batman does not fight crime alone. His most helpful allies include Alfred the butler, sidekick Robin, and Police Commissioner Gordon. Other heroes such as Superman and Batgirl make many appearances as well.

Villains

Everyone wants a piece of the Batman, from the certifiably insane Joker to the genetically modified Mr. Freeze. When he's not battling supervillains, he is taking on mobsters like Carmine Falcone. Then there is Ra's al Ghul and the League of Shadows, who are constantly trying to destroy Gotham.

Media

Although Batman began as Detective Comics written by Bob Kane, the icon has expanded to several different medias. Batman has been featured in both live action and animated film and television, making it one of the largest science fiction franchises in history.
